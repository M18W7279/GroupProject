What is yatsuhashi?

Yatsuhashi also referred to as otabe is a traditional japanese sweet and a specialty of kyoto.

Its made using a thin square piece mochi (japanese rice cake) which is then folded diagonally into a triangle and stuffed with a sweet filling (traditionally red bean paste).

They are made with all kinds of different flavors of mocchi and filling
